About Me

Aerospace engineer turned Agile Coach & Stand-up Comedian. I deconstruct complex org dynamics using system logic, and rebuild team connection using storytelling and humor. No boring slides, no corporate theater.

From Aerospace Engineering to the frontlines of Agile transformation — I spent years watching brilliant strategies die because the humans behind them were never brought along for the ride. Now I fix that with actionable structures and the high-energy delivery of stand-up comedy sets.
